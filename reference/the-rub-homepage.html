Our story

Kitchen, not a lab.

We tried building this brand as a science experiment once — molecule logo, lab coats, the whole bit. Turns out the truest version was already sitting in the fridge: a deli tub, a strip of painter's tape, and a name that didn't need explaining. So we kept the part that mattered — testing every batch until it was right — and dropped the part that didn't.
